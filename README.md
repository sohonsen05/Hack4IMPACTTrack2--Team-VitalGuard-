## Domain
AI & Automation in Smart Healthcare

## Problem Statement
Clinical AI chatbots give confident medical advice based solely on text prompts, completely ignoring a patient's real-time physiological state. A nurse may describe symptoms verbally while the patient is simultaneously showing critical vitals like low SpO2 or tachycardia — the AI never sees this. This "confidence trap" was identified by ECRI as the #1 health technology hazard for 2026. Patients face life-threatening risk when AI recommendations contradict what the hardware is actually measuring. There is no existing safety layer that cross-checks AI output against live sensor data before it reaches a clinician.
